Vijay

On May 10, 2026, C. Joseph Vijay officially took the oath as the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu, beginning his first term in public office. He is an Indian politician and founder-leader of Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam, who entered electoral politics after a highly successful career in the Tamil film industry. Widely known as Vijay, he emerged as a major political force in the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly election through a campaign centered on governance, welfare, and youth outreach. As of 2026, his assumption of office as Chief Minister marked a historic transition from cinema to state leadership, establishing him as one of the most prominent new political figures in contemporary Tamil Nadu politics.
